# Activity 1: Personalize ChatGPT

Use this activity to set up ChatGPT so it gives answers that match your work, tone, and preferences.

Copy the sample below and replace the text inside `[ ]` with your own details.

## 1. Nickname
- [Your preferred name]

**Sample:**
```text
Kavindu
```

## 2. Occupation
- [Your job title and organization]

**Sample:**
```text
Administrative official at the Board of Investment of Sri Lanka
```

## 3. More About You
- [I often need help with...]

**Sample:**
```text
I often need help with drafting official emails, preparing meeting notes, summarizing documents, explaining procedures clearly, creating reports, and communicating with investors, colleagues, and other government institutions.
```

## 4. Interests, Values, or Preferences
- [I prefer answers that are...]

**Sample:**
```text
I prefer clear, concise, professional, and practical answers.
Use bullet points when helpful.
Avoid unnecessary jargon.
If something is uncertain, say so clearly.
```

## 5. Custom Instructions / Additional Behavior
- [Write instructions for how the AI should respond to you]

**Sample:**
```text
When answering work-related questions, use a professional and respectful tone suitable for government and investor-facing communication.
For emails, letters, and notices, make the writing formal, polite, and easy to understand.
For summaries, highlight key points, required actions, deadlines, responsible parties, and follow-up items.
For explanations, use simple language and step-by-step guidance where useful.
If facts, regulations, procedures, or policies may have changed, mention that they should be verified with the latest official source.
```

## Test Prompt

After adding the personalization details, try this prompt:

```text
Draft a professional email to an investor explaining that their submitted documents have been received and are under review.
```
